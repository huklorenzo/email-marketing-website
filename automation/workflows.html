Email Automation Workflows

Strategic automated sequences that drive engagement and revenue

1. Welcome Series

A 5-email sequence designed to onboard new subscribers and set expectations.

Email 1: Welcome (Immediate)

Trigger: New subscriber signup

Goal: Confirm subscription, set expectations

Content: Welcome message, what to expect, preference center link

Email 2: Brand Story (Day 2)

Trigger: 2 days after signup

Goal: Build brand connection

Content: Company mission, values, founder story

Email 3: Value Proposition (Day 4)

Trigger: 4 days after signup

Goal: Showcase key benefits

Content: Top 3 benefits, feature highlights, social proof

Email 4: Special Offer (Day 7)

Trigger: 7 days after signup

Goal: Drive first purchase

Content: Exclusive discount code, best-selling products, urgency

Email 5: Engagement (Day 10)

Trigger: 10 days after signup

Goal: Maintain engagement

Content: Blog posts, resources, community links

Expected Impact: Improved completion rates and higher engagement vs. non-automated campaigns

2. Abandoned Cart Recovery

A 3-email sequence to recover abandoned shopping carts and drive conversions.

Email 1: Reminder (1 hour)

Trigger: Cart abandoned for 1 hour

Goal: Gentle reminder

Content: "You left items in your cart", product images, direct CTA

Email 2: Social Proof (24 hours)

Trigger: Cart abandoned for 24 hours

Goal: Create urgency with social proof

Content: "Others are buying", reviews, limited stock warnings

Email 3: Final Offer (72 hours)

Trigger: Cart abandoned for 72 hours

Goal: Last chance with discount

Content: Discount code, expiration notice, final CTA

Expected Impact: Improved cart recovery rates and recovered revenue

3. Post-Purchase Follow-up

A 4-email sequence to enhance customer experience and drive repeat purchases.

Email 1: Order Confirmation (Immediate)

Trigger: Purchase completed

Goal: Confirm order, set expectations

Content: Order details, shipping timeline, tracking info

Email 2: Shipping Notification (When shipped)

Trigger: Order status changed to "shipped"

Goal: Provide tracking, build excitement

Content: Tracking number, estimated delivery, care instructions

Email 3: Delivery Confirmation (After delivery)

Trigger: 1 day after delivery

Goal: Request review, offer support

Content: Delivery confirmation, review request, support link

Email 4: Cross-sell (7 days after delivery)

Trigger: 7 days after delivery

Goal: Drive repeat purchase

Content: Complementary products, related items, loyalty program

Expected Impact: Improved review rates and repeat purchase behavior

4. Re-engagement Campaign

A 3-email sequence to re-engage inactive subscribers and improve list health.

Email 1: "We Miss You" (90 days inactive)

Trigger: No engagement for 90 days

Goal: Reconnect emotionally

Content: Sentimental message, what's new, preference update

Email 2: "What's New" (120 days inactive)

Trigger: No engagement for 120 days

Goal: Showcase new products/features

Content: New arrivals, product highlights, limited offer

Email 3: "Last Chance" (180 days inactive)

Trigger: No engagement for 180 days

Goal: Final attempt with strong offer

Content: Exclusive discount, unsubscribe option, preference center

Expected Impact: Improved re-engagement rates and better list health

Best Practices

  • Always include clear unsubscribe and preference center links
  • Test optimal send times for each workflow
  • Personalize content based on user behavior and data
  • Monitor performance and adjust timing/content as needed
  • Set up suppression lists to avoid sending to engaged users
  • Use dynamic content to show relevant products/content
  • Track conversion goals for each workflow
