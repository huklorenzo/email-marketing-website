Testing Methodology

A/B testing is essential for optimizing email performance. Tests should be conducted across subject lines, email content, CTAs, send times, and design elements. All tests should follow statistical significance requirements (high confidence level, minimum sample size per variant) to ensure reliable results.

Best Practices

  • Test one variable at a time for clear results
  • Ensure statistical significance before making decisions
  • Document all test results for future reference
  • Test continuously - optimization is an ongoing process
  • Consider segment-specific testing for better insights
  • Use test results to inform future campaign strategies
