## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Email Marketing Success

1. **Segment your audience** - Personalized content performs significantly better
2. **Test everything** - A/B test subject lines, content, CTAs, and send times
3. **Automate strategically** - Use automation to nurture leads and recover carts
4. **Monitor metrics** - Track KPIs and optimize based on data
